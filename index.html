See my CV (pdf) or Resume for complete experience and credentials. Email me at greg@pronghorns.net.

Current and past projects

  • Helping scientists publish and make use of environmental data at Environmental Data Initiative
  • Ecology, data science, and informatics at the Jornada Basin LTER program
  • Studying soil carbon cycling and impacts of solar power development in the Mojave Desert with UC Berkeley colleagues
  • Ecohydrology research at the New Mexico Elevation Gradient cluster of AmeriFlux sites
  • My dissertation research studying seasonally snow-covered ecosystems in the western U.S.
