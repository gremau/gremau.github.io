So...Matthew has his own blog now. Whatever.

So, Matthew Utley stopped posting on this blog and went out and got his own blog.

Fine Matthew.

Do whatever you want, Matthew, but don't forget that your blogging career started here at this humble blog. I hope I don't crawl out of a dumpster in 5 years and see you walking down the street being followed by a herd of adoring fans. I hope you don't look over and see me in my greasy rags, stinking of selenium, muttering about Polygala subspinosa, then turn to your fans and whisper "I used to blog with him...but he... he kind of lost it...", then shake your head and walk away.

You better do a guest post this summer.

Whatever Matthew...
