### I am the master of my domain {#i-am-the-master-of-my-domain .post-title}

::: post-body
::: {style="clear:both;"}
:::

I purchased a domain on the internet. Land is cheap out there on the
internet these days. If you visit my domain, which is called
www.primaryproductivity.org, at this exact moment you will find nothing
substantive, just some links and ads from GoDaddy.com (the registrar
that I purchased my name from) that serve as a kind of placeholder until
I get a host and some of my own content built there. I don\'t know what
I\'ll do in this domain. Probably I\'ll start posting my work there - so
these blogs will probably move over there eventually, and hopefully a
new blog on my graduate research experiences will develop, and I can
keep interesting electronic media and info there for people to access. I
hope it will all come together as something interesting over time.\
\
The name\.... is dorky, but I like it, and if you don\'t like it you
should stop and think about this: Primary productivity is defined as the
movement of carbon from the atmosphere into living organisms over time.
Essentially, it is the rate of photosynthesis, and 99% of all life on
earth depends on the products of this directly or indirectly. This
process is performed mainly by vascular plants on land and by algae in
the oceans. You, and all the other beings that grow and reproduce by
eating plants and other animals are another result of primary
productivity. So, what I\'m saying here is that this blog is about YOU.
And me. Its about [us]{style="font-style: italic;"}.
