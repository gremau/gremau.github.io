I Feel Horrible - a play in one act

HER: I feel horrible.

ME: Let me help!

HER: I still feel horrible.

ME: I'll do anything. Marry me!

HER: I'm starting to feel better.

ME: (stares blankly into the distance)

HER: Lets go have some fun with our friends.

ME: I feel horrible.

(offstage laughter)
