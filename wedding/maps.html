Maps and Directions to the Wedding

 

The ceremony and reception places are just off of I-5 in southwest Oregon (near Grant's Pass and Ashland).

If you live near the the I-5 corridor, directions to this area are simple. From Northern California head north

on I-5. If you live in northern Oregon, Washington, or Canada head south on I-5. The exit for the cabin is

exit 66, the "Hugo" exit, about 11 miles north of Grant's Pass. Ashland is 35 miles south of Grant's Pass.

Several maps are linked below. First are full-page versions of the maps included in the invitation. These are

what you'll need for detailed directions to the ceremony and reception.

If you are coming from Salt Lake City, there are two routes your can take, "Vanilla" and "Black Rocky (Desert) Road."

The "Vanilla" route is longer, but safer, and there are ample services along the way.


View Larger Map

The "Black Rocky (Desert) Road" is shorter, but only advisable if you can handle long stretches (150 miles+)

between service areas and are comfortable changing a flat in the howling wastelands of northern Nevada.

Bring extra pemmican and rifle ammunition.
